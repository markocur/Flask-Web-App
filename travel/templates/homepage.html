Why Journalling?

As nicely put by writer Christina Baldwin, a journal “allows us to leave a collection of still lifes that records our personal journeys”. Writing in a journal seems to address a fundamental human desire — self-expression. It also helps to reflect on the events that shape our lives. And travelling can have a huge impact on your live!
